Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Fostering innovation through digital transformation, renewable energy, and sustainable development practices.

Sustainability

Promoting sustainability through renewable energy, eco-friendly practices, and balanced economic and environmental growth.

Infrastructure

Building resilient infrastructure with modern technology, sustainable design, and enhanced connectivity for inclusive growth.

Empowerment

Driving empowerment through education, digital inclusion, skill development, and opportunities for all communities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to revolutionize the nation through technology-driven growth, renewable energy, and sustainable practices. By fostering inclusivity, enhancing digital transformation, and building robust infrastructure, it envisions becoming a global model for innovation, development, and environmental responsibility.

Digital Transformation

Accelerating digital transformation through innovation, smart technologies, inclusive access, and enhanced connectivity for growth.

Green Energy

Promoting green energy initiatives through renewable sources, reducing carbon footprints, and fostering environmental sustainability.

Modern Infrastructure

Developing modern infrastructure with advanced technology, sustainable designs, improved connectivity, and resilience for future growth.
